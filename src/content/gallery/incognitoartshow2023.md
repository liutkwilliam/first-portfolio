Selected photos of can-did photography from the Sale Day of Incognito Art Show 2023 in Paddington NSW, Sydney on 15 July 2023

Incognito Art Show is a charity art show, with all A5-sized artworks contributed from artists in any levels of expereinces (professionals, hobbyist, students, young artists, children) are shown on the Sale Day and online sale for $100 Australian Dollars each, all profits go towards in supporting programs for artists living with disabilities, the benefiting studios are studio A and Little Orange.

The photos are published in Incognito Art Show's website, social media including Facebook, Instagram and LinkedIn.
